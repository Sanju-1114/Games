How to Play Ludo

  • 🎲 Each player has 4 pieces starting in their home base
  • 🎯 Roll a 6 to move a piece out of home onto the starting square
  • ➡️ Move pieces clockwise around the board
  • ⚔️ Land on an opponent's piece to send it back home
  • ⭐ Safe squares (colored) protect your pieces
  • 🎁 Rolling a 6 gives you another turn
  • 🏆 First to get all 4 pieces to the center wins!
